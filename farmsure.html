About Farmusre

Farmusre is a platform designed to connect landowners and farmers. We simplify the contract farming process by allowing users to create accounts, upload necessary documents, and access direct market opportunities.

Key Features

User Accounts

Create accounts for both farmers and landowners.

Document Upload

Upload land ownership and agreement documents securely.

Market Access

Access direct markets to sell produce and negotiate deals.
